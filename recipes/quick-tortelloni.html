Antero's Quick Tortelloni

This is a fast one. Just some ready made tortelloni, oil, onions, garlic and spices.
Simple and extremely tasty!

Ingredients

  • Tortelloni of your choosing
  • One onion
  • Garlic (at least a couple cloves)
  • Spices - herb mixtures work great
  • Olive oil

Time

About 10-15 minutes

Instructions

  1. Chop the onions and garlic
  2. Put water to boil
  3. Add some salt to water
  4. While the water is heating, add copious amounts of oil to a pan, turn the heat
  5. Add the chopped onions, garlic and spcies to the pan.
  6. Let the good stuff simmer in the oil
  7. When the water is boiling, add the pasta and drain afterwards
  8. Put the pasta back to the kettle, pour the oil and other goodies on top of the pasta
  9. Stir the kettle
  10. Serve with parmegiano cheese

Tip:
For variety - you can add some bacon, meatballs or other such things to the oil and spices.
